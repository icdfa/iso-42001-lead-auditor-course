## About This Course

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping every sector of the global economy -- from healthcare diagnostics and financial lending to criminal justice and public administration. With this transformation comes profound responsibility. **ISO/IEC 42001:2023** is the world's first international standard specifying requirements for an **Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS)**, providing organizations with a structured framework to develop, deploy, and govern AI responsibly.

This **Lead Auditor Training Course** is the most comprehensive self-paced preparation available for professionals seeking to plan, conduct, report, and follow up on ISO/IEC 42001 certification audits. Every module has been written to the depth of a professional textbook chapter, combining rigorous standard interpretation with practical, real-world application.

> *"Responsible AI governance is not a compliance checkbox -- it is a continuous commitment to ensuring that AI systems serve humanity with fairness, transparency, and accountability."*
> -- International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this course, you will be able to:

- **Interpret** every clause of ISO/IEC 42001:2023 in the context of an audit engagement.
- **Plan** a risk-based audit program for an AI Management System from initiation to closure.
- **Conduct** Stage 1 (document review) and Stage 2 (on-site) audits using professional techniques.
- **Gather evidence** through document review, interviews, and observation of AI system operations.
- **Write nonconformity reports** using the professional PLO (Provision-Location-Objective Evidence) structure.
- **Evaluate corrective action plans** to ensure they address root causes, not just symptoms.
- **Manage** complex audit situations including defensive auditees, technical complexity, and ethical dilemmas.
- **Apply** the standard to diverse AI systems across healthcare, finance, public sector, and retail.

## Assignments

Two graded assignments are provided to test your ability to apply the standard in practice.

---

### [Assignment 1: Scenario-Based Audit Simulation](resources/assignment_1_scenario_based.md)
**Type:** Practical Audit Simulation | **Marks:** 100 | **Time:** 4-6 hours

You are the Lead Auditor conducting a Stage 2 certification audit of **MediScan AI Ltd.**, a healthcare technology company whose AI diagnostic tool is deployed in 47 hospitals. You are given 7 detailed evidence items and must produce professional audit deliverables.

| Task | Description | Marks |
| :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Task 1** | Classify 7 evidence items and write full PLO nonconformity reports for each NC found. | 40 |
| **Task 2** | Write a formal Audit Conclusion and make a justified certification recommendation. | 20 |
| **Task 3** | Review two corrective action plans -- accept or reject with full justification. | 20 |
| **Task 4** | Prepare a structured closing meeting agenda and speaking notes. | 20 |

---

### [Assignment 2: Research-Based Critical Analysis](resources/assignment_2_research_based.md)
**Type:** Academic Research Report | **Marks:** 100 | **Word Count:** 3,500-5,000 | **Time:** 8-12 hours

Select a real-world AI governance failure (e.g., Amazon's biased recruiting AI, the COMPAS recidivism algorithm, or the UK A-Level grading algorithm) and produce a critical, evidence-based analytical report.

| Section | Description | Marks |
| :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Section 1** | Introduction and organizational background. | 10 |
| **Section 2** | Critical analysis of the failure -- root causes, stakeholder impacts, legal consequences. | 30 |
| **Section 3** | ISO/IEC 42001 gap analysis -- at least 5 clause failures and 2 Annex A control gaps. | 30 |
| **Section 4** | Proposed 18-month AIMS implementation strategy for the organization. | 20 |
| **Section 5** | Critical evaluation of whether ISO/IEC 42001 alone is sufficient for AI governance. | 10 |

## Certification Pathway

Completing this repository prepares you for formal ISO/IEC 42001 Lead Auditor certification. The typical pathway is:

| Stage | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1. Self-Study** | Complete all modules, resources, and assignments in this repository. |
| **2. Formal Training** | Attend a 5-day accredited Lead Auditor training course from a recognized body (e.g., PECB, BSI, Bureau Veritas). |
| **3. Examination** | Pass the proctored Lead Auditor examination (typically 3 hours, 80 questions). |
| **4. Work Experience** | Demonstrate the required audit experience (varies by certification body). |
| **5. Certification** | Receive your ISO/IEC 42001 Lead Auditor certificate and maintain it through CPD. |

> **Note:** Completion of this repository alone does not confer formal certification. It is designed as comprehensive preparation material to maximize your success in the formal examination and practical audit work.
